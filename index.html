One thing I think will go back to the way it was before COVID-19 is work and school. People will return to their jobs, and children will go back to school in person, not online. Additionally, I think normal travel will resume. People will still take airplanes over cars because of the convenience, and the airline industry will recover eventually.

After this pandemic is over, I think people will value their time much more. I would like to think that people will spend more time outside, or more time with their friends. People that have grown into a rythm of going to work and then home and back to work will hopefully find something to fill their time. I also believe that it will change the way that large companies cover their employees with health insurance, and most companies will provide the option to work from home.
